The Problem We See

A major problem for enforcement teams is to know where and when to patrol in order to plan effective rounds.Existing alternatives are based on traditional statistics like averages per hour, day, or zone. However, this approach focuses on a simple historical analysis of the data taken out of context and out of the dynamic environment of the city. Moreover, the amount of data required is prohibitive to work finely on logistic planings per street and per hour. Finally, these methods are often carried out by hand, which makes the work tedious, unreplicable, subject to miscalculation, and misinterpretation.

How Park.AI helps

To overcome these constraints, Park.AI will utilizes machine learning algorithms to predict where and when the number of violations is highest, and hence prescribe the areas to visit. The Algorithm is therefore able to determine the ideal route to optimize parking enforcement
